**THE ENGLISH POEM:**

Oh what a wondrous day I've sniffed and snuffed,
Comparing cities, wind and weather aloft.
Aalborg's breeze is brisk, a biting frost in air,
Dance and prance with glee, without a care.
The wind howls strong, a force so free and bright,
Copenhagen's gusts, are almost as cold as night.

But wait, for Kathmandu's warmth does rise,
Gentle sunbeams kiss my fur, and open my eyes.
A tropical haven, free from chills and fright,
A place to run and play, under golden light.
The wind whispers soft, a lullaby so sweet,
A place to dream and sleep, with paws at rest.

As I ponder and think, which one I'd adore,
I know my heart's desire, is a sun-kissed shore.
Tomorrow I'll chase the sun, to Kathmandu's land,
To dance and play, in a tropical paradise so grand.

**THE NEPALI POEM:**

(विनती है, एक छोटी सी परिचित)

माया तपाईले खुसी पारे जाना यो,
खगेना के बारेमा सुनि बुझी, खुसी पारे,
आलगवा, कोपेनहाग्न, नेपाल भने,
तीन शहर हुन, तीन तापमान, तीन वातावरण.

आलगवा तापमान केहि ठण्डो, तिमुरुनै खुसी,
वातावरण सुहाए, हिउँच्यो न्याय,
केहि ठण्डा ठण्डा, फ्रिस्टि हिउँच्यो न्याय.

कोपेनहाग्न यो ठिकाना, तपाईको नाक पानि,
चिसो वातावरण, ठण्डो हिउँच्यो न्याय

नेपाल यो भुक्यो भुमि, खुसी पारे जाना यो,
माया, माया, तपाईको बारेमा सुननै,
तपाईले खुसी पारे जाना यो,
माया, माया, तपाईको हुन यो,

माया, माया, माया, नेपाल भन्यो,
खुसी पारे जाना यो ने, माया भन्यो।
