**The English Poem:**

(As I stroll the moors, lost in melancholy)

Fair Aalborg, city of northerly storms,
Where rainless days give way to chill, sharp winds,
A biting frost creeps in, with winter's mournful form,
And though the sun shines bright, 'tis but a distant gleam.

Copenhagen's afternoons, a warm and languid spell,
Invite the senses to partake in gentle, summer's tale,
But as the evening falls, the air grows cool and still,
And night's melancholy whispers secrets, lost in misty will.

And Kathmandu, the land of snow-adorned peaks,
Where rain-laden skies bespeak a drama, vast and unique,
A howling gale, a tempest's wild and savage might,
Betrays a realm of contrasts, dark and light, in endless fight.

As I roam, lost in thought, I feel a pang within,
A yearning for fair skies, and calm, still air to spin,
If fate would grant me tomorrow, to wander by the sea,
Perhaps in distant Aalborg, the moors' chill whispers will draw me.

**The Nepali Poem:**

(As I wander, lost in thought, on Kathmandu's streets)

नेपालको मान्छे, नेपाली हाम्रो घर,
चाँडा बाटो फेरी जाय जिने खेल, मुन्छे जातो देख,
जाडो ठुलो, ठुलो बुङगुरो, हालो कुराँसे हुँदै काँध,
अँडो धुपले माटो हुँदै पोख्री, खेलती खेलती लुकले निस्कन्छ।

चाँडो मान्छे, दुःखी होला जस्टो,
मान्छेले ठुलो बुङगुरो हुँदै ग्यालको गली,
दुःखी मान्छे चाँडो ठुलो हुँदै, ग्याँसो रोइ जस्तो,
मान्छेले ठुलो बुङगुरो हुँदै, केही नै मार्छ।

काठमाडौँको गोरा चाँडो फेरी, मेछेको फेरी,
अँडो धुपले सुनो हुँदै, काँटेदार खेत,
बुङगुरो हुँदै, ग्याँसो रोइ जस्तो,
हाम्रो किरण खेल्न भिन्नो भिन्नो ठीकै हो

हामी माँगेले मुन्छे मोर्मोरो,
ठीकै हो ग्याँसो निस्कन्छ मुन्छे भोली भोली,
थपारो हुँदै, फोरो हुँदै, ग्याँसो रोइ जस्तो,
अरु देख्छु बिनो हुँदै, मुन्छे भोली भोली निस्कन्छ।

(If I could be elsewhere tomorrow, I would seek the city where fair winds blow gently.)
