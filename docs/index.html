In twilight's hush, where shadows dance and play,

**THE NEPALI POEM:**

कालो फाग्नाको ठाउँ
तिमी लाई कहाँ छोड़दिन?

आलबोर्ग महादेवको ठाउँ
बिस्कोये नगुना जति हो

कोपेनहेगनको ठाउँ
बहुत सुकुम्भो जति हो

काठमाडौको ठाउँ
गौरी-बिल्वको हृदय जस्तो हो

आलबोर्गको ठाउँ
तपीहुन सेती चुलोँ छ

सँध्याको टिम्रो कोहरा
सपना जस्तै बुझाउँछ नगुना

कोपेनहेगनको ठाउँ
नैँटो जस्तो फिसल्यो मान्छेको चश्माएँ होल

गुम्सो, फुसफुसाइँछ
जित जागो पाहड़मा हो यस्का क्याँदी तारैँ

काठमाडौको ठाउँ
जलजलित ब्रह्मको घुघरा जस्तो छ

ओह, मैले जातै भाँचेको
यस फाग्नाको ठाउँमा सो कसार छोड्यो नैँ

Translation: (Please note that translating a poem is an art, and nuances may be lost in translation.)

Where the fog of fall lies,
Where will you leave me?

Aalborg, the city of the great god,
Is cold and gray like the color of frost.

Copenhagen, the city of the great beauty,
Is so clear, like the eye of a young man.

Kathmandu, the heart of the beautiful goddess,
Is like the sacred flame burning in the heart.

Aalborg, the city in the darkness,
Is as hot as the fire burning in the cold.

The mist at sunset, as gentle as the dream,
Fades the world's beauty, leaving only emptiness.

Copenhagen, the city in the evening,
Falls asleep, like a gentle child.

It is so quiet, so still,
Like a forest on a cold winter morning.

Kathmandu, the city in the storm,
Is like the sound of the drum beaten by the Lord of the universe.

Oh, I have left my heart burning,
In that city, in the fall, I will come back tomorrow.
