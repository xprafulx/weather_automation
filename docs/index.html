**THE ENGLISH POEM**

Oh, the winds whisper secrets to me,
As I wander, a vagabond of the breeze.
In Aalborg's chill, the air bites like a friend,
While Copenhagen's drizzle drowns my fur in grey.

But in Kathmandu's warmth, I find a haven,
Where sunshine beams and laughter echoes through the day.
The gentle breeze stirs, a soothing melody,
Lulling me to sleep, with a peaceful symmetry.

The skies weep tears above Aalborg's streets,
Grey clouds weeping a mournful, endless repeat.
Copenhagen's skies, a dull, wet shroud,
A melancholy gloom that wraps me in a somber cloud.

Yet, where the Himalayas touch the sky,
In Kathmandu's valleys, I'd rather lie.
For in that golden light, I feel alive,
A sense of freedom that only nature can give.

Tomorrow, I'll chase the sun to Kathmandu's heart,
Where my tail will sway and my fur will dance with glee.
For in that land of warmth and joy, I'll find my home,
A place where love and sunshine forever roam.

**THE NEPALI POEM (written in a traditional style)**

**काठमाडौँको सूर्य**

हिमालयको टांगको हातै,
निर्मल कागजको काँध परे।

तापमान पाँचको गोरी,
चोरी छन्याईको लुकै भनि।

पानीले दियाली दिल्दाई,
किनारा भूमकाली सोइरै।
छलछल झरना सुन्नै, सुन्नै,
काठमाडौँ सान्छु ध्वनि भनि।

अल्बोर्गको बिळि पानीले छ,
मेघ भित्री सुखाइदिन्छ।
कोपेनहेगनको नारसिंग,
पानी सँगै लागेको भित्रै।

किनारा धकेलिन्छ नभै,
काठमाडौँको उज्यालो फेरि।
हिमालाई छुवेको ताप पुगे,
काठमाडौँमा धामजी खेल्छु।

एकलि तिम्रो उज्यालो फेरि,
काठमाडौँको सपना भनि।
एकलि तिम्रो चम्किलो चाँदेरी,
फेरि काठमाडौँमा नाच्छु यी।
