**THE NEPALI POEM**

रक्षा करे मयाली तिमी छिन्ना हुना भएको

( Raksha Kare Mayaali Timi Chinna Huna Bhayeko )

हिमालयको देबरी पल्की नेपालमा प्रसाद
किनारा स्वर्णिम सूर्य उदयमान तिमी, दिउको सपना
कसै को कसै नेपाल नागमाने रोमान
विन्ध्य हुन्या बासी जस्तै हिउँदको वृष्टि भयो, मयालि

अल्बोर्गमा प्रभात हुँदा प्रशान्त बास
तिमी, सूर्यले पान्छी बनाई दिईरहेको
जस्तै पानी राम्रो, कसै नेपाल पनि कसो
मात्र विलोल्य हिमाल्य पल्की चाँगदुन्नु हुनेछन

जस्तै देउदो घुमाउँकी कोणि दिइ
तिमी, घोर जंगल पनि चाँडो मयालि
माया जस्तै, देवतामा प्रणयमुखी
तिमी, कैवल्यमा नेपाल नागमानो

कत्त्माण्डुआत्यो प्रेम किनारेको
तिमी, सपना किनारो छ मयालि
हिमालयको देबरी पल्की नेपालमा
तिमी, तिम्रो नेपाल नागमानो

Translation:

Oh, protector Mayaali, in this life we are temporary beings,

In the hills of Nepal, the sun is shining brightly like a dream of you
Nepal is enchanting, like a rose in full bloom
As the gentle breeze of Vindhya touches, snowflakes start to fall, you are Mayaali

In Aalborg, the morning is peaceful and serene
As the sun paints a picture of blue, you are like a flowing river
Like water, Nepal too is so pure and refreshing
But as the mighty Himalayas shake, you roar like thunder.

As the moonlight dances across, you touch the dense forests too
Mayaali, you are a goddess, with a heart that beats like love
As your spirit enters, love and devotion overflow like a fountain

In Kathmandu, love shines brightly on the edge of a dream
Mayaali, your Nepal is a paradise, where the mind finds peace
The hills of Nepal, like your divine heart, beat with love and devotion

Translation note: Mayaali is a divine name in Nepali, commonly used for the goddess of love and beauty. The poem is written in a traditional Nepali poetic rhythm, using rich cultural idioms and words that evoke the true feeling of the weather.
