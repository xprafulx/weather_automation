**THE ENGLISH POEM:**

O dearest moors, how I do stray,
With weather-tempered moods that come to stay.
Aalborg, fair city by the bay,
Basks in warmth that doth my soul enliven in May.

The air, a gentle caress, doth whisper low,
A soothing zephyr that doth my heart bestow
With peace and calm, as evening's hush doth creep,
And stars appear in velvet-blackening deep.

But Copenhagen, stony bastion of the land,
Doth stand as sentinel to northern winds' command,
Whipping flags, and whipping hearts with fierce delight,
As gales do howl, and stormy nights do take their right.

And then, far off, in distant Eastern skies,
Kathmandu doth rise, a golden, sun-kissed surprise,
A tropic haven where the sun doth beat its might,
And warmth, a balmy lover, whispers through the night.

Oh, to be tomorrow where the breeze is light,
In Aalborg's tranquil gardens, bathed in golden, dewy light!
Where morning sunrise doth creep, unseen, as the night doth fade,
And the whole world wakens, born anew, in radiant, smiling shade!

**THE NEPALI POEM:**

( Translated to English )

Aalborg ka jaga, kati pahar ma,
Khusi bhav, hamra jiwan ma,
Aayo, dharti bhitara, bhuja bhitara,
Phool bhare jangali ko khel bhitara.

Jungal ko khusi ka prabhaat, dhoondhu,
Nirjan jungal ko pranaam, karanu,
Bansuree ma, khushi ka pranaam,
Nirdosh bhumi, kushi bhume, hamra aashirwaad.

Copenhagen, mauni jagat hai,
Hamaare khatre mein chadhi hui hai,
Udaari kati jahaad, hamaare jeevan mein,
Bhala ya bahi, khushi-kushi, hamesha bhi chadh jaata hai?

Kathmandu, jaga, tyaag ki kahanai,
Jeevan kee raah, hamaaree aayein,
Tyaag, khushi, aashay, sab kuchh aaya hai,
Aiseee mehke mehak, hamra dil chaaya hai.

Kalkein, Aalborg jaga chaliyaa, mehki bhumi,
Kati pahar ma, khushiyon ki kahaan,
Jungle ko khusi, jal bhi bahaati,
Aur mehki bhumi, khushi bhumi ko jal bhi bah jaati.

In English:

Oh, Aalborg's scene, at afternoon time,
Joy fills, our lives, like the world's rhyme.
A place, where earth and sky meet, full of play,
Where forest blooms, with flowers, in dance.

The quiet forest, pays homage to our presence, naturally.
In the forest wind, joy's greeting is made,
The quiet earth, is our wish, forever made.

Copenhagen, a silent world, is fortified,
A protective shield, against life's battles fought.
A fierce wind howl, in our life's journey,
A joyful cry, in pleasure, forever echoes forth.

Kathmandu, a world of selfless giving,
A path, of life, for us, is unfolding.
Selfless giving, joy, hope, all come together,
A warm and sweet breeze, that touches our heart.

Tomorrow, I'd go to Aalborg's scene,
In the gentle land, where joy's melodies are seen,
The forest is full of joy, the river flows too,
And the gentle land, is our joy, forever true.
