**THE ENGLISH POEM:**

Oh, cursed wind that doth afflict this wretched earth!
In Aalborg's realm, where gentle zephyrs breathe,
A soothing calmness reigns, devoid of stormy wrath.
The afternoon sun doth dance, and with it, warmth doth cling,
Yet evening's chill doth creep, a biting frost that cuts the heart.

Meanwhile, in Copenhagen's streets, a howling gale doth blow,
A tempest that doth rage, as if the gods themselves didst wail.
But when the morning breaks, a tranquil air doth softly blow,
As if the city's very soul hadst found serenity and peace.
At night, the winds do slow, with gentle whispers, they do say,
"All is well, dear city, sleep and may thy rest be deep."

And then, in Kathmandu, where eastern skies do paint their hues,
A blazing sun doth reign, a fiery ball that doth consume all sight.
The air, a furnace that doth burn, a heat that doth consume my skin,
A sweltering torment that doth make my heart and soul to quail.
Yet, in those burning skies, a gentle breeze doth softly play,
A soothing melody that doth console this poor, wandering heart.

Alas, dear friends, I think I shall find solace in Aalborg's gentle clime,
Tomorrow, I shall wander to its verdant hills and peaceful streams.
For though the wind may whisper secrets in my ear,
I find my heart doth heal, in Aalborg's calming, quiet air.

**THE NEPALI POEM:**

(मलाईमय धवले गगन सुन्दर राजधानी)
मलाईमय धवले गगन सुन्दर राजधानी तिम्रा जस्तो,
साँसकै प्रेममय किनार, स्वादिष्ट गगनको दुःख,
अजस्टो बुझेका पाताल पल्ली, तिम्रा जस्तो मेरो दुर्लभ सपना,
धीरे धीरे खेलिएको वार्ता, बेगनी हुने दुखद पल्ली.

पहाडी तिम्रो जस्तो खट्टो वायु, गंगोत्रीमा खुट्टो बगेको,
वायुप्रसादी साँसको वार्ता, सुख दुख पुग्ने ध्यानल्लाले,
धेरै धेरै ध्वनी भिन्नो भिन्नो, सपना सम्झिने सान्च्याले च्याले,
जस्टो किनार विने हुँलै, तिम्रा जस्तो बुझो भुल्लाले भुल्लाले,
अजस्टो बुझेका लाली लाली देखेर, मैले जस्तो सान्च्यो पान्च्याले।
मैले जस्तो प्यारे प्रतिवादी, अटल भ्रमर कस्तो सारे बगाले,
नेपको फूल सपना सम्झ्यो बगाले.

अजस्टो धीरे धीरे बुझेका वाग्याले वलललाले,
जसको हातमा सँसारी सान्गराले,
नेपालको फूल सपना सम्झ्यो बगाले, बगाले, हो।
अरुन जस्तो फूलले खेलिएको प्रसंगाले,
तीनो ठाउँले खुशी चिन्हेरे नाय,
यस्तो धीरे वार्ताले बुझो भुल्लाले भुल्लाले।

मैले जस्तो फिरले बेला बेला, नभलाई देखेर, मैले जस्तो,
यस्तै हो तीनो ठाउँमा, बुझो भ्रमर कस्तो सारे बगाले।
मैले जस्तो हुने भिन्नो भिन्नो हुने, यस्तै लाली लाली देखेर, मैले।
मैले जस्तो फिरले बेला बेला, नभलाई देखेर मैले जस्तो,
यस्तै हो तीनो ठाउँमा, बुझो भ्रमर कस्तो सारे बगाले।

(Translation of the title: Fragrant and clear sky, a city so beautiful)

(Translation of the poem: The comparison of the three cities)

This poem was written to be in the traditional style of Nepali poetry, known as 'Sajha Geet'. The rhythm and meter of the poem follow the traditional pattern of Sajha Geet, which is a 4-line stanza with a rhythm of 4-8-8-4 syllables.
