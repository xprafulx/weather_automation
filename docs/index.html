**The English Poem**

A Symphony of Skies

As twilight descends upon three distant shores,
I find myself lost in a dance of elements.
Aalborg's gentle breeze rustles my hair,
while the scent of damp earth wafts through the air.

But Copenhagen's warmth envelops me like a lover's caress,
softly whispering secrets in my ear.
The wind stirs, a gentle melody that beckons me to stay,
as petals unfold, revealing the beauty of the day.

And then, Kathmandu, a realm of mystic charm,
where heat and mist entwine, and the air is alive with form.
Rains fall like tears from the heavens, purifying and cleansing the land,
and in the darkness, stars appear like diamonds in the hand.

Tomorrow, I would flee to Kathmandu's mystical veil,
where the earthy fragrance of monsoon rains makes my heart sing and sail.
I long to be enveloped by the mystery of its skies,
where raindrops fall like blessings from above, and my soul takes flight, free from disguise.

**The Nepali Poem (in a style inspired by the great Nepali poet Laxmi Narayan Tripathi)**

काठमाडौँका हावापानीको राग

जाँदा नै चमनमा खेलिने तिम्रो सारा बारिस
जिमको तिमीले जुनु भयो जसले तिमीलाई
गुलाबो सारा बारिस मात्रै

अल्बोरग जति, वातावरण तिम्रो
पाँखनो हुँसानी सारा प्रेममायो
कल्प्ने नै थापिरहेको किनारो

कोपेनहागनका तिम्रो जोशो
मस्तिस्कमा छ्याउने एक प्यास जस्तो
आवाजले केहि भन्नु पर्दैनन्

तिम्रो विशाल बारिस रे जुनको सारा
मी किनारो जाने भनिनन् थोडा निस्को

जस्तो कि साँझको सपनो मेरा नजिक आ
काठमाडौँले सुरुवै बारम्बार जाने के हुन्छ
किनारो मेरो जति खेलिन्छ
चमचमिने हावापानि मेरो

—End of The Nepali Poem—
