**The Nepali Poem:**

(Sung in a melancholy, wailing melody)

काठमाडौँको तिमिलो हावामा,
तिम्रो सूर्यो फैलन्न जङ्गलकम,
कुरबो किन्हारा साँस्कृतिक,
प्रेमको हृदयको मार्मिक चेतना,

काँडा काठमाडौँका फाल्गुनको प्रभावी,
बहिरे ज्याँदै नेपाली संस्कृति,
सार्को तिम्रो रङ्ग, तिम्रो बुझावी,
बासमधी निरीक्षण, विवक्षालाई खोलावी,

अट्रिस्तो काठमाडौँको शीतको भुवन,
तिम्रो फाल्गुनलो सुक्रियो ज्ञान,
चिरब्रह्मद्वारीमा भक्ति, भयंकर,
साँकेत्यात्रा, जोगी, तिम्रो निरीक्षण में,

अपूर्णो काठमाडौँको मायो हृदय,
तिम्रो सूर्यो प्रकाश दिएर दिसाई,
क्रुद्धो, अन्तरात्मा प्रकाश दियो,
ब्रह्मविद्या, द्योतित दुरभिसंध्या में, मेरी कामना तिम्रो प्रसादमा लेख्न पाइ।

Translation:

In Kathmandu's warmth, your sun spreads like a jungle,
Your fragrance spreads, our cultural scent.
Heart's pain, your pain, your sorrow, your blissful awareness,

Crisp Kathmandu's April effect,
Outside spreads Nepal's culture, your color, your understanding,
Deep and subtle, a perception, a discovery within,
Exploring the unexplored, a yearning within, an awakening.

In Aalborg's cold, a world's darkness,
Your winter brings bitter knowledge, a harsh awareness,
Chirping, a Brahmin temple's worship, a fear, a doubt's shadow,
Your observation, my sight within, my heart's inquiry, within.

In Kathmandu's unfinished heart, your sun shines,
Your warmth brings awareness, your light dispels darkness,
Furious, an inner light shines, a brahma vidya, insight, within,
A glimpse of hidden reality, a promise, a wish, your kindness.

(Note: Nepali poetry is known for its simplicity and depth, rich imagery and cultural idioms, and this poem tries to reflect that with a melancholic and introspective tone)
