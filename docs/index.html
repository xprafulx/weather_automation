**TRANSLATION OF THE NEPALI POEM**

The radiant city of Kathmandu
its shadow is the night of the new moon
the bank of the river, a calm reflection
the nights are dry, with no cloud in sight

In Aalborg too, there is a reflection
the nights are calm and still, with no wind or cloud
the bank is filled with the radiance of the lamp
as if the light has awakened from its slumber

In Copenhagen, a dark and stormy night
its bank is restless, as if it has been awakened
the night is spent, and day has begun
but the dark night still lingers in its wake

There are other times and places beyond this,
but those I would like to be in the company of
In Kathmandu, the nights are calm and peaceful
with a warm and gentle light that banishes the darkness

In Kathmandu, the nights are cold and dry
but still, I find solace in its calm
the night of the new moon is a time of calmness
as if the darkness has been washed away

In Aalborg, the nights are calm and still
with a soft and gentle light that fills the heart
why should a person need darkness and light
when the world is full of such contrasts?

In the night of the new moon, the darkness is complete
but that is when the light begins to shine
In Kathmandu, the nights are calm and peaceful
with a warm and gentle light that banishes the darkness

In Kathmandu, the air is filled with a warmth
that is beyond words, a light that shines so bright
that is why I would like to be in Kathmandu's company
with its calm and peaceful nights.
