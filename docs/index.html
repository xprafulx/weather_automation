**THE ENGLISH POEM:**

"Rain's Lullaby"

In Aalborg's grey, where shadows play,
A somber silence holds the day.
The chill of dawn, a biting frost,
Awakens hope, as morning's mist is lost.

Copenhagen's veil, a gentle mist,
Dances with wind, a soothing kiss.
A soothing drizzle, a melancholy hue,
Weaves a tapestry, both old and new.

But Kathmandu's heat, a sweltering fire,
Unleashes life, as clouds conspire.
A torrential downpour, wild and free,
Echoes the beat, of a city's glee.

As night descends, the city's skin,
Glistens with rain, like a lover's kiss within.
Aalborg's darkness, a somber shroud,
Copenhagen's lights, a shimmering cloud.

But I'll follow the rain, to Kathmandu's heart,
Tomorrow, where the monsoon's melody will part.
In the city's heat, I'll find my home,
Where rain and wind, will be my lullaby's tone.

**THE NEPALI POEM:**

(Transliteration: निस्टितलको भेष)

(Verse 1)
काश्मीरी खेलमा काठमाडौं,
आनी के खाँदे पाहाँले,
गगनले भेष, साँस्कृतीक रामरा
अरुण ज्वाला ल्या पाहाँले

(Translation: In Kathmandu's festive spirit,
The wind whispers secrets, a cultural treasure)
(Kathmandu is a city of vibrant festivals,
A place where cultural heritage meets the warmth of love)

(Chorus)
निस्टितलको भेष, साँस्कृतीक भेष,

बिहार भित्र, सुखमय भेष,
सारांशमा मान्दे काँयले ज्वाला

(Translation: In the spirit of the monsoon,
The cultural heritage comes alive, like a green bud)
(In the land of Nepal, the rainy season is a time of joy,
A soothing balm for the soul, a reminder of the beauty of nature)

(Verse 2)
