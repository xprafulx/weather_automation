**THE NEPALI POEM (मा के पोएतमा **

कास्तोँ जसकै भिन्न हुन् रायो,
तेरे तिम्रो पार्कमा निश्छल हुन्
लकडिआँ गुण्डुङ पलाएँ,
तपिलो वातावरणमा।

जस्तोँ खलासोँ बाँझोँ पानी बिचर्नो,
तसरी जस्तोँ कोहरानी हुन,
मात्कालो भातको माझ हुन
तसरी हन्नसँसँगी लहान हुन।

जस्तोँ वातावरणमा जस्तोँ को भातो, रे,
कास्तोँ भिन्न प्रस्थिति हुन् वास,
सोँसँगी म हाम्रै का हुन नेपाली
अमृत महल हुन प्रस्थिति जस्तो का हुन।

सो रे गाउँमाझ म हेरे त्याङ,
योँ पालोँ रायो सुकिन्छ कि,
कास्तोँ होँ योँकै प्रस्थिति बिचारि,
हुँमाझ भावमा भातोँ त्यो यात्रा।

कास्तोँ जस्तोँ मात्कालो भात,
तसरी हन्नकै मारामारा त्यो हुन,
सो रे कास्तोँ हुन रायोको भिन्न हुन
म हाम्रै जस्तोँ सुकिन्छ हुन।

Translation of THE NEPALI POEM:

The three cities differ in their atmosphere, 
The trees in the park sway gently,
The biting cold in the environment.

Just like the water of a river, just like 
The mist in the sky, the love has grown
Into a small flame, just like the warmth of the sun,
The three positions of the three cities differ.

The three atmospheres differ just like that,
The three positions are different, O nepali,
The heaven of nectar is different, the position of life is just like this.

So, I see in my heart that,
This day, all the clouds are departing,
Just by thinking about the three positions,
The heart is filled with that journey.

Just like the water of the lake,
The waves in the sea, that is the way, 
Just like the three clouds differ in the sky,
So too, the three positions differ.

The three cities differ just as much, 
Just how the love of the heart grows, just how 
The three positions of the cities differ, 
So the way of our heart departs.
