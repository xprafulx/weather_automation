**THE ENGLISH POEM**

"Oh, moors, thou art a wistful friend indeed,
As I roam here, lost in sorrow's tide.
Amidst the grey skies, I chanced to find
Three cities, each with fate entwined.

Aalborg's warmth, a gentle caress,
Did soothe my soul, as morning's dawn's dress.
The sun's soft touch, a comfort to my heart,
Did chase the chill, a fleeting, sweet depart.

But Copenhagen's breeze, a mournful sigh,
Did whisper secrets, as the winds did fly.
A biting frost did grip the town's cold frame,
And in its darkness, I did see a sorrow's stain.

And Kathmandu, oh fairest Nepalese shore,
Did call to me, with whispers of a love before.
The gentle drizzle fell, like tears from above,
And in its quiet, I did find a peaceful, endless love.

Tomorrow, I'll flee this somber air,
And seek the balmy touch of Kathmandu's gentle lair.
For in its warmth, I know my heart will find
A peace, a solace, a love left behind."

विपरीत चासो, र विपरीत दम्राले भए नै हो।

कोेबेनहैगन् ले प्राणी हराउँदै, वायुमा रानी निस्किरै,
छोरो चिसो ठाउँमा छ्याउँदै, जुनि के री तपाईं छनि।
प्रेमकाे लाग्दै यौगकमी बाह्र के री, धीमो धीमो हालिरै।
अर्थी कति बिर्साे किन्तु नेपाली हाेनले नै हाे।

काठमाडौँ ले ज्वारिमा चिरिराने प्यारलै, भस्मितालि बाह्र कै,
जसले हामीलाई यात्तिराने छायाेनि हाेन तपाईरा।
आँखी कैले चाँपिएला, ठोरे ह्यै प्रेमकाे ले,

भवेश्वर प्रेमकाे ली माेन, हारै ठाउँ जानी काथ,
